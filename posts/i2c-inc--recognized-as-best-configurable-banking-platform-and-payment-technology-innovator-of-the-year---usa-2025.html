i2c Inc. Recognized as Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025

i2c Inc., a leading provider of digital payment and banking technology, has been named the Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025 by Pan Finance, a leading financial news and analysis organization. This prestigious award recognizes i2c Inc.'s innovative and highly configurable banking platform, which enables financial institutions to quickly and easily deploy new payment products and services.

The award is a testament to i2c Inc.'s commitment to delivering cutting-edge technology solutions that meet the evolving needs of the financial industry. The company's platform is designed to be highly flexible and scalable, allowing banks and other financial institutions to rapidly develop and launch new payment products and services, improving customer experience and driving business growth.

"We are thrilled to receive this recognition from Pan Finance," said Amir Wain, CEO of i2c Inc. "Our team has worked tirelessly to develop a platform that is not only highly configurable but also provides a seamless and secure payment experience for consumers. This award is a validation of our efforts and reinforces our position as a leader in the payment technology industry."

i2c Inc.'s platform has been widely adopted by banks, credit unions, and other financial institutions across the globe. The company's technology enables the issuance of credit, debit, and prepaid cards, as well as the provision of digital wallet and mobile payment services. With its robust and scalable architecture, i2c Inc.'s platform can handle high volumes of transactions, making it an ideal solution for large and small financial institutions alike.

The award from Pan Finance is the latest in a series of recognitions that i2c Inc. has received for its innovative technology and commitment to excellence. The company has been named as one of the fastest-growing companies in North America by Deloitte and has received numerous awards for its technology and innovation.

As the payment industry continues to evolve, i2c Inc. is well-positioned to remain at the forefront of innovation, providing financial institutions with the technology and expertise they need to stay ahead of the curve. With its highly configurable platform and commitment to delivering exceptional customer experiences, i2c Inc. is set to continue its growth and success in the years to come.
