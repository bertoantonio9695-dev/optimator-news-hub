i2c Inc. Wins Top Honors as Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025

i2c Inc., a leading provider of digital payment and banking solutions, has been named the Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025 by Pan Finance, a renowned global finance publication. This prestigious award recognizes i2c's innovative approach to payment technology and its commitment to delivering flexible, scalable, and secure solutions to the financial industry.

The award is a testament to i2c's cutting-edge platform, which enables banks, fintechs, and other financial institutions to launch and manage a wide range of payment products and services. With its robust and agile architecture, i2c's platform allows clients to configure and deploy payment solutions quickly, reducing time-to-market and improving customer satisfaction.

"We are honored to receive this recognition from Pan Finance, which validates our dedication to innovation and excellence in the payment technology space," said Amir Wain, CEO of i2c Inc. "Our platform is designed to meet the evolving needs of the financial industry, and we are committed to delivering solutions that are both secure and scalable."

i2c's platform has been widely adopted by financial institutions and fintech companies worldwide, who appreciate its flexibility, reliability, and ability to support a wide range of payment products and services. With its modular architecture and open APIs, the platform allows clients to integrate seamlessly with existing systems and third-party services, reducing implementation costs and improving overall efficiency.

As a Payment Technology Innovator of the Year, i2c Inc. has demonstrated its commitment to pushing the boundaries of innovation in the financial industry. The company's focus on delivering secure, scalable, and flexible solutions has earned it a reputation as a trusted partner for banks, fintechs, and other financial institutions looking to stay ahead of the curve in the rapidly evolving payments landscape.
