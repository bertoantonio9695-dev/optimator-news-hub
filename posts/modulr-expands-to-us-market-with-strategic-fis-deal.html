Modulr Expands to US Market with Strategic FIS Deal

Modulr, a renowned UK-based FinTech company, has officially announced its expansion into the US market. This strategic move is made possible through a significant partnership with FIS, a global leader in financial technology. The deal marks a significant milestone for Modulr, enabling the company to bring its innovative payment solutions to the US market.

Modulr has gained recognition for its cutting-edge payment infrastructure, designed to simplify and accelerate payment processes for businesses. By integrating its technology with FIS's extensive network, Modulr aims to revolutionize the way payments are made in the US. The partnership will enable Modulr to leverage FIS's vast expertise and reach, facilitating the company's entry into the highly competitive US FinTech landscape.

The US market presents a substantial opportunity for Modulr, with its vast and diverse economy offering immense scope for growth. By partnering with FIS, Modulr is well-positioned to capitalize on this opportunity, bringing its unique payment solutions to a broader audience. The deal is expected to drive innovation and competition in the US payments sector, ultimately benefiting businesses and consumers alike.
