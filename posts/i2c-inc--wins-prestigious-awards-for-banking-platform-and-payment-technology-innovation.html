i2c Inc. Wins Prestigious Awards for Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovation

i2c Inc. Named Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025 by Pan Finance

i2c Inc., a leading provider of digital payment and banking solutions, has been recognized as the Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025 by Pan Finance, a renowned industry publication. This prestigious award is a testament to i2c's commitment to innovation and excellence in the payment technology sector.

The award recognizes i2c's cutting-edge platform, which enables banks, fintechs, and other financial institutions to quickly and easily launch new payment products and services. i2c's platform is highly configurable, allowing clients to customize their offerings to meet the evolving needs of their customers. With its robust architecture and scalable design, i2c's platform has become the go-to solution for companies looking to stay ahead of the curve in the rapidly changing world of payments.

"We are thrilled to receive this recognition from Pan Finance," said Amir Wain, CEO of i2c Inc. "Our team has worked tirelessly to develop a platform that is both innovative and reliable, and it's gratifying to see our efforts acknowledged by the industry. We will continue to push the boundaries of what is possible in payment technology, and we look forward to working with our clients to bring new and exciting solutions to market."

The Payment Technology Innovator of the Year award is a testament to i2c's commitment to innovation and its dedication to staying at the forefront of the latest trends and technologies in the payment space. With a strong focus on research and development, i2c is constantly exploring new ways to enhance its platform and deliver more value to its clients.

According to Pan Finance, the award is based on a rigorous evaluation process that takes into account factors such as innovation, customer service, and market impact. i2c's award is a reflection of its exceptional performance in these areas and its ability to make a significant contribution to the payment technology industry.

With this recognition, i2c Inc. solidifies its position as a leader in the payment technology sector, and the company is poised for continued growth and success in the years to come. As the payment landscape continues to evolve, i2c is well-equipped to meet the changing needs of its clients and stay ahead of the competition.
