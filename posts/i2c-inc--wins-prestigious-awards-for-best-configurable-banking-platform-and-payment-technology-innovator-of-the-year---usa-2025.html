i2c Inc. Wins Prestigious Awards for Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025

i2c Inc., a global leader in digital payment and banking technology, is proud to announce that it has been named the Best Configurable Banking Platform and Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025 by Pan Finance, a renowned financial news and awards organization. This prestigious recognition is a testament to i2c's commitment to innovation, customer satisfaction, and its ability to deliver cutting-edge payment solutions that meet the evolving needs of the financial services industry.

The award for Best Configurable Banking Platform recognizes i2c's agile and flexible platform, which enables financial institutions to quickly and easily configure their payment solutions to meet the unique needs of their customers. This platform has been praised for its scalability, security, and ease of use, making it an ideal solution for banks, credit unions, and other financial institutions looking to stay ahead of the competition.

The Payment Technology Innovator of the Year – USA 2025 award acknowledges i2c's dedication to pushing the boundaries of payment innovation. The company's team of expert engineers and researchers work tirelessly to develop new and innovative payment solutions that utilize the latest technologies, such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and blockchain. These innovations have enabled i2c to stay at the forefront of the payment industry and provide its customers with a competitive edge in the market.

"We are honored to receive these prestigious awards from Pan Finance," said Amir Wain, CEO of i2c Inc. "This recognition is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our team, who are committed to delivering innovative payment solutions that meet the evolving needs of the financial services industry. We will continue to push the boundaries of payment innovation and provide our customers with the best possible solutions to stay ahead of the competition."
